## Recording protocol (per pass-2 decision #2 — Julia Child method)

1. **Pre-record terminal execution** — ScreenStudio captures all three terminals + the live ledger + the live portal. No mistakes; no awkward pauses; no waiting on RPC.
2. **Live voiceover** — recorded in Descript on top of the silent capture. Tad's voice, conversational, no "uhh."
3. **The 100x banner** sits on top of the capture for the full 90 seconds.
4. **The portal video** auto-renders the IMAGE + GOLDEN CODEX panels for the final 10 seconds.

If a real Kite Discord question lands during the live demo session, Tad can pause and answer — but the recorded video is the artifact that gets submitted.
